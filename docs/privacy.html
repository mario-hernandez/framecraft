Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2025

FrameCraft ("the App") is a developer utility designed to create marketing screenshots. We believe in privacy by design.

Data Collection

No Data Collected

FrameCraft works 100% offline on your macOS device. The App does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data, usage analytics, or telemetry.

  • We do not tracking your usage.
  • We do not have access to your screenshots (they remain on your local disk).
  • We do not require user accounts or login.

External Services

The App contains links to third-party websites (e.g., GitHub) for documentation and installation instructions. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.
